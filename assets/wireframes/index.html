Ottawa's Best Peanut Free Custom Cakes and Sweets!

Welcome to Designed to be sweet! We are a newly formed small Ottawa bakery that is absolutely delighted to share our love of baking with this wonderful city. Family owned and run we enjoy greeting all our clients with the sweet smell of custom baked goods. Don’t be fooled by our newly formed status we have main years of baking passed down through generations within our small family. Making us perfect for anyone craving a taste of mom’s baking.

What makes Designed to be Sweet different? Why would you choose our sweets? It’s very simple; we love baking and new challenges in baking and because of this we are completely custom to each and every customer. We will always have featured flavours, but what we truly enjoy doing is helping our clients design and mold their dream cake. We work with different tools to make cakes and cupcakes that will both stun the eyes and mouth. Best of all we are very sensitive to peanut allergies and make all of our bake goods peanut free.
