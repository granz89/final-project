Designed to be sweet is a completely custom bakery peanut free bakery. Don’t be limited by everyday flavours, if you can think it we will find a way to make it work. Feeling a little vanilla? Want to mix it up some carrot? We can do that! The only exception within this bakery is that this is a peanut free facility; so all creations will be part of this.

To see samples and be inspired please visit our photos page where we have a variety of cakes and cupcakes to look at. We are not only custom in our flavours, but in our designs as well. We love to create beautiful cakes and specialize in memorizing fondant creations. From bridal shower to our popular violin cake don’t be shy to make your request.
